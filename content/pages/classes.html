Traditional Training in Kathak

Our Kathak classes are led by our Founder and Artistic Director Lawanya, disciple of Pt. Jai Kishan Maharaj. The classes are designed to meet the specific goals of each student, be it beginner, intermediate or advanced level.

Classes are conducted with special emphasis given to the tradition of the Lucknow Gharana of Kathak. Holistic training is given to develop different aspects of the art form - inexplicable bhav, mesmerising footwork, graceful movements, brisk spins and so much more.
