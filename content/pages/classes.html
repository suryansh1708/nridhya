Kathak Dance

Kathak is one of the most elegant dance forms of India. It revolves around the concept of storytelling. An art form with inexplicable charm, mesmerising footwork, and graceful movements, Kathak figures among the eight Indian classical dance forms.

It charms and captivates people with its swift pirouettes, unique rhythmic virtuosity and lyrical exploration of romantic as well as devotional poetry. The sharp yet fluid movements synchronised with deft and brisk spins, unleashes a visual splendour onstage.

Artistic Mentorship

Under the guidance of Lawanya, disciple of Pt. Jai Kishan Maharaj and Founder & Artistic Director of Nridhya, students are trained in a disciplined yet inspiring environment that encourages dedication, creativity, and artistic growth.

The teaching approach blends traditional guru–shishya values with structured training suited to modern learners, ensuring that students develop both technical excellence and a deep appreciation for the art form.

Structured Learning

Students are also provided preparation for certified annual examinations conducted by Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya Mandal, giving them the opportunity to progress through recognised levels of classical dance education.

In addition to regular classes, students are encouraged to participate in stage performances, productions, and cultural events, allowing them to gain valuable performance experience and confidence.

Choreography

Elevate your events with our exceptional choreography services. We specialise in creating captivating dance routines tailored to your specific event. Whether it's a corporate function, wedding reception, or special celebration, we will bring your vision to life through the power of dance.

Nridhya

The vision with which Nridhya was founded is deeply rooted in a commitment to celebrate, preserve, and nurture the rich diversity of India's cultural and artistic heritage. Conceived as a space where tradition and creativity coexist harmoniously, Nridhya seeks to uphold the timeless values of Indian classical arts while engaging with the evolving sensibilities of the contemporary world. Through its work, the institution aspires not only to keep these traditions alive but also to allow them to grow, adapt, and flourish for future generations.

At its core, Nridhya is dedicated to research, exploration, and the dissemination of knowledge in the field of Indian performing arts. The institute offers rigorous training and thoughtful artistic mentorship, drawing upon the depth of traditional pedagogy while embracing innovative and modern approaches to learning. By blending the discipline of the guru–shishya parampara with creative experimentation, Nridhya fosters an environment where students are encouraged to develop both technical excellence and individual artistic voice.

The curriculum at Nridhya is designed to be integrated, diverse, and immersive, enabling students to evolve not only as skilled performers but also as thoughtful artists and cultural ambassadors. Through a combination of regular classes, workshops, performances, and collaborative explorations, students are guided to expand their understanding of movement, rhythm, expression, and storytelling. This holistic training nurtures confidence, creativity, and a deep sensitivity towards the cultural narratives embedded within the art forms they practice.
