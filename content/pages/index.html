About Lawanya Gupta

Lawanya Gupta is an acclaimed international-level Kathak dancer known for her breathtaking footwork, emotive expressions (Abhinaya), and deep spiritual connection to the art form. She has performed on prestigious stages worldwide, bringing the rich heritage of Kathak to a global audience.

Nridhya Kathak Institute

Founded by Lawanya, Nridhya is a premier institute dedicated to preserving and propagating the pure tradition of Kathak. We offer comprehensive training for all ages, focusing on technique, theory, and the soul of classical dance.

  • Workshops & Masterclasses
