About Lawanya Gupta

Lawanya is a disciple of Pt. Jai Kishan Maharaj. She is a committed Kathak dancer and artistic director of Nridhya with a deep understanding of Indian classical dance traditions. Her artistry is deeply rooted in the illustrious tradition of the Lucknow Gharana.

Over the past decade, she has had the privilege of performing in prestigious venues across the world and creating innovative dance productions.

Her commitment to the art extends beyond performance and she has always pushed herself to further her scholarly understanding of the art form. She holds a Bachelors of Performing Arts in Kathak from Indira Kala Sangeet Vishvavidyalaya.
